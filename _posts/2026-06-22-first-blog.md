I have been meaning to start writing on this website for a while now, and today is the day! I thought it would be a great way to share my thoughts, experiences, and ideas with the world. So here we go — welcome to my blog!

## A Little About Me

My name is Aufa Riduan Cahyadi. I'm passionate about **backend development**, **network infrastructure**, and **system automation**. Currently, I work in a university computer lab where I manage and maintain servers and lab facilities.

Outside of work, I'm a dedicated **homelab enthusiast**. I spend my free time self-hosting applications, managing virtualized environments with Proxmox, deploying services via Docker, and configuring secure networks using WireGuard and Tailscale. There's something deeply satisfying about building your own infrastructure from scratch!

## What to Expect

On this blog, I plan to write about:

- 🖥️ **Homelab adventures** — documenting my self-hosting journey, from Proxmox setups to Docker deployments
- 🌐 **Networking** — tips and tricks on VPN configurations, firewall rules, and network automation
- 💻 **Backend development** — things I learn while working with Go, PHP, and databases
- 🤖 **Automation** — scripts, tools, and workflows that make life easier
- 📝 **General tech musings** — thoughts on the tech world and things I find interesting

## Why Blog?

I believe in learning in public. Writing things down not only helps me solidify my understanding, but also (hopefully) helps others who are on a similar journey. Plus, it's a great way to look back and see how much I've grown.

## Stay Tuned

This is just the beginning. I have a lot of topics I'm excited to explore and share. If any of the topics above interest you, stick around — there's plenty more to come!

Thanks for reading, and welcome to my corner of the internet. 🚀
